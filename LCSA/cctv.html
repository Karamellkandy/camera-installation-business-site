Types of CCTV Cameras

Dome Cameras

Ideal for indoor use, dome cameras offer a wide-angle view and are often designed to blend with the surroundings, making them suitable for discreet monitoring in retail stores, restaurants, and offices. Their durable casing makes them resistant to vandalism.

  • Wide Coverage: Effective for large indoor spaces.
  • Vandal-Resistant: Durable dome cover protects against tampering.
  • Discreet Design: Blends with indoor decor for subtle monitoring.

Bullet Cameras

Designed for outdoor use, bullet cameras offer a long-range field of view, making them suitable for monitoring perimeters, parking lots, and open spaces. Their shape is highly visible, which can act as a deterrent for criminal activity.

  • Weatherproof: Built to withstand various weather conditions.
  • Long-Range Visibility: Captures clear images over long distances.
  • Deterrent Effect: Visible presence deters intruders.

PTZ Cameras

Pan-Tilt-Zoom (PTZ) cameras provide maximum control, allowing users to pan horizontally, tilt vertically, and zoom in on specific details. This flexibility makes them ideal for monitoring large areas that require a high level of detail, such as stadiums, warehouses, and event spaces.

  • Remote Control: Adjust the camera angle and zoom remotely.
  • High Flexibility: Covers wide areas with a single camera.
  • Zoom Capability: Focus on details without losing clarity.
